A Research Cognition Toolkit

`$phd` is not a title simulator or a fixed paper-writing workflow. It is a composable toolkit for systematic research thinking.

这里的 PhD 是什么意思

这里的 PhD 指一种研究工作风格：持续建立背景，比较相关工作，抽象复杂信息，形成认知框架，产生 insights 和 hypotheses，并愿意不断修正。

原则

可组合

研究动作彼此交织，因此 skill 提供模块，而不是强制单一路径。

结构化

复杂信息应被整理成矩阵、timeline、taxonomy、因果模型、actor map 和 question tree。

可持续

有价值的研究应能被后续复用，因此默认的长期产出是静态 HTML 网站。

证据敏感

区分事实、推断、解释、假设、猜测和开放问题。

能力意图

  • 收集和整理 background information。
  • Survey 并比较 related work。
  • 为概念或课题建立简洁认知框架。
  • 对复杂信息进行总结、抽象和扩展。
  • 按模块、时间、因果、历史、人物、组织和相关工作梳理。
  • 生成 insights 和 hypotheses。

What PhD Means Here

Here, PhD means a research style: continuously build background, compare related work, abstract complex information, form cognitive frameworks, produce insights and hypotheses, and remain willing to revise.

Principles

Composable

Research actions are intertwined, so the skill provides modules rather than one mandatory path.

Structured

Dense information should become matrices, timelines, taxonomies, causal models, actor maps, and question trees.

Durable

Useful research should be reusable in later sessions, so the default durable output is a static HTML site.

Evidence-Aware

Separate facts, inferences, interpretations, hypotheses, speculation, and open questions.

Capability Intent

  • Collect and organize background information.
  • Survey and compare related work.
  • Build concise cognitive frameworks for concepts or topics.
  • Summarize, abstract, and expand complex information.
  • Structure topics by module, time, causality, history, people, organizations, and work.
  • Generate insights and hypotheses.
