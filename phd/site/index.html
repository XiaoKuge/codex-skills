PhD Skill

`$phd` 是一个面向人和 agent 的可组合研究认知能力包。本网站用于持续记录它的设计、用法和演化。

`$phd` is a composable research cognition toolkit for humans and agents. This site records its design, usage, and evolution.

当前状态：`$phd` 已增加整合进 research site 的研究讲解视频能力，可输出课题解构、脚本、分镜、shot list、工具方案和剪辑计划。

目标

`$phd` 帮助 Codex 做系统化研究思考：整理背景信息，比较相关工作，形成认知框架，总结和抽象复杂信息，产生 insight 与 hypothesis，并持续 enrich living research site。

Purpose

`$phd` helps Codex perform systematic research thinking: collect background, compare related work, form frameworks, summarize and abstract complex information, generate insights and hypotheses, and enrich living research sites.

Core Capabilities

CapabilityWhat it doesReference
OperatorsResearch actions: background mapping, survey, abstraction, synthesis, gap finding, and hypothesis generation.operators.md
LensesDimensions for structuring topics: module, time, causality, history, actors, methods, incentives, governance, and failure modes.lenses.md
